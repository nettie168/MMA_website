University of York

Mixed Martial Arts Club

Welcome

Welcome to the University of York Mixed Martial Arts club. We are a University sports club that trains and competes in mixed martial arts. We welcome everyone, whether you're a complete beginner or experienced. Some students come for the social atmosphere and exercise, others for the opportunity to compete in cage fights twice a year. However you train, you're welcome at the club.

Classes

Our classes are split into three (plus an extra one for women only) including:

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Thai Boxing and Wrestling.

Each class focuses on a different aspect of mixed martial arts. You can attend one session or all of them, it's up to you.

Coaches

We have a number of coaches who take different sessions throughout the week

Ian Calliou

Wrestling

Ian is our external wrestling coach, with a wealth of experience having trained and competed in Canada. He is one of our excellent external coaches from one of the York gyms.

Dave Phillips

Thai Boxing

Dave is an external coach from Legions Gym in Clifton Moor, where is has turned complete beginners into World Champions. He is also the referee for our boxing fight nights.

Harry Edwards

Wrestling and MMA

Harry is all-round MMA fighter, skilled in both striking, wrestling and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. He won his first amateur cage fight in the first round with a flying arm bar.

Tom Smith

Striking

Tom has a black belt in taekwondo, getting a bronze medal at the Commonwealth Games. He took the the title fight at the last Fight Night, winning by TKO.

Nettie A-Borrill

Women's and Non-binary classes

Nettie has trained in martial arts since they were 11, obtaining a black belt in karate at age 18. They have competed in MMA, boxing, thai boxing, jiu jitsu and karate.

George Mason

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

George has dedicated himself to Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and is one of the rising stars in the club. He has a passion for ensuring everyone learn the best of BJJ.
