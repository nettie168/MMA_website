There are three different ways to pay for sessions: Annual, Termly and per session. Annual Membership is £80, Termly is £30 and Pay-As-You-Go is £2.50 per session. If you plan to go to every session then Annual Membership is the most cost effective, but if you can only make a few sessions then Pay-As-You-Go is cheaper.
